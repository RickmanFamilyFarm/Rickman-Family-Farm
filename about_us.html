ABOUT US

Our family started a farm for more easier acces to the residents on Camano Island and Stanwood. We had an idea for a non-GMO production in our town, helping growing our town in benefically ways. We first started in the year 2023 doing everything homemade and naturally, coops, healthy food, and distributing our products to homes.

For breakfast and lunch we let the free-ranged chickens eat the grass and the worms and all what comes with nature. Then when its dinner, they are fed with Organic Grower Crumbles bythe company Scratch & Peck. The store where we get all of our supplies, to feed and nurture them is the Country Store in Stanwood.

The owner of the company is Kadrian Rickman, he saw an opportunity to help people in our town and to be an honest company that wants for you to eat healthy. Him and his family, the Rickman's came to Stanwood because they saught a window of possibility and a chance to help better the community and for the future generations of children.
